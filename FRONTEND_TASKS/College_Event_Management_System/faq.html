Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our events

To register for events, follow these simple steps:

  1. Click on the Registration link in the navigation bar
  2. Fill in your personal details including name, roll number, branch, email, and mobile number
  3. Select the events you wish to participate in by checking the corresponding checkboxes
  4. Review the terms and conditions and check the agreement checkbox
  5. Click the Submit Registration button
  6. You will receive a confirmation email with your registration details

Most of our events are free for all registered students. However, some premium events may have a minimal registration fee:

  • Technical Quiz: Free
  • Hackathon: Free (includes meals)
  • Poster Presentation: Free
  • Coding Contest: Free
  • Project Expo: Free
  • Cultural Fest: ₹100 (Includes entry + refreshments)

Payment instructions will be provided during registration for paid events.

Yes, absolutely! You can register and participate in multiple events simultaneously. However, please ensure that:

  • You check the event schedule to avoid timing conflicts
  • You can attend multiple events on different dates
  • For events on the same day, verify if participation in both is possible
  • You register for each event separately

Most students participate in 3-4 events during the event season. Choose wisely based on your interests and availability!

Please bring the following documents to the event venue:

  • Student ID Card: Mandatory for verification
  • Confirmation Email: Printed or digital copy on your mobile
  • Valid ID Proof: Aadhar card, Passport, or Driving License
  • For Hackathon: Laptop/Desktop and charger (bring backup power bank)
  • For Poster Presentation: Printed poster (if required by event guidelines)

Important: Students without a valid student ID will not be allowed entry. Please carry it without fail!

Yes, registration can be cancelled with the following terms:

  • Before 7 days of event: Full refund (if applicable)
  • 3-7 days before: 50% refund
  • Within 3 days: No refund

To cancel registration:

  1. Email us at events@college.edu with your roll number and event name
  2. Include a brief reason for cancellation
  3. Allow 3-5 business days for processing

Note: No-shows will not be eligible for refunds in future events.

We keep you updated through multiple channels:

  • Email: Detailed information will be sent to your registered email
  • SMS: Important updates sent to your mobile number
  • Website: Check Events page for latest updates
  • Social Media: Follow us on Facebook, Instagram, and Twitter for live updates
  • College Portal: Announcements posted on official college portal

Make sure your contact information is correct during registration to receive all updates!

Some events support team registrations:

  • Hackathon: Team of 2-4 members (one member registers for the team)
  • Project Expo: Individual or team (2-3 members)
  • Coding Contest: Individual participation only
  • Cultural Fest: Team events (2-5 members) for performances

For team events:

  1. One member registers on behalf of the team
  2. Provide names and roll numbers of all team members
  3. The registered member is responsible for team coordination
  4. All members must carry valid ID cards at the venue

In case of event rescheduling:

  • You will be notified via email and SMS immediately
  • New date and time will be provided
  • Your registration remains valid for the new date
  • You can choose to participate on the new date or request a refund

Reasons for rescheduling:

  • Unforeseen circumstances or emergencies
  • Severe weather conditions
  • Technical issues or infrastructure problems

We always try to give at least 48 hours notice for rescheduling. Thank you for your understanding!
